Who we are

Madge Inc. was started in 2005 for the procurement, installation and commissioning of equipment for the production of rolled and processed metals.

The Madge Inc team has experience for over 200 projects. From complete greenfield plant installations to specific equipment upgrades.

All types of metals and alloys are produced on our projects, from Battery Lead, to Precision Stainless Steel foil, Bonded / Clad Metals, Carbon Steel Roofing and Deep Drawing Aluminium. Madge Inc can install, commission, operate and service the equipment.

Our success has been to listen to our customers and to provide first class service while completing the projects on time.

Madge has delivered consistent results year after year. You get the experience of the best people in the business working alongside you to meet and exceed your expectations.

Looking forward to hearing from you!

George Gerard
