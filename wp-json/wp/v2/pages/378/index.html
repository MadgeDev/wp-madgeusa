Madge Inc. was started in 2005 for the procurement, installation and commissioning of equipment for the production of rolled and processed metals.<\/p>\n

The Madge Inc team has experience for over 200 projects. From complete greenfield plant installations to specific equipment upgrades.<\/p>\n

All types of metals and alloys are produced on our projects, from Battery Lead, to Precision Stainless Steel foil, Bonded \/ Clad Metals, Carbon Steel Roofing and Deep Drawing Aluminium. Madge Inc can install, commission, operate and service the equipment.<\/p>\n

Our success has been to listen to our customers and to provide first class service while completing the projects on time.<\/p>\n

Madge has delivered consistent results year after year. You get the experience of the best people in the business working alongside you to meet and exceed your expectations.<\/p>\n
